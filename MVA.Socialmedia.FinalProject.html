Social Media Data Analysis

Project Overview:

This project investigates the effects of social media use on our sleep, productivity, and mood. We’re examining data to determine whether there’s a relationship between users’ emotional states or sleep quality and the amount of time they spend on social media sites like Instagram or Twitter. The objective is to determine whether various online behaviors are associated with better or worse feelings and what lessons can be drawn from that.

Data Dictionary

  • character: Name or identifier of the individual/person.
  • Instagram: Activity level on Instagram (e.g., time spent, interactions).
  • LinkedIn: Activity level on LinkedIn.
  • SnapChat: Activity level on Snapchat.
  • Twitter: Activity level on Twitter.
  • Whatsapp/Wechat: Activity level on WhatsApp or WeChat.
  • youtube: Activity level on YouTube.
  • OTT: Activity level on Over-the-Top platforms (e.g., Netflix, Hulu).
  • Reddit: Activity level on Reddit.
  • Trouble_falling_asleep: A measurement that might be graded on a scale (0 for no trouble, 1 for some difficulties) to indicate whether the person has difficulty falling asleep.
  • Mood Productivity: The person’s attitude, maybe assessed using a scale (1 being extremely negative and 5 being very positive)..
  • Tired waking up in morning: A measure indicating how tired the individual feels upon waking up in the morning, possibly rated on a scale (e.g., 0 for not tired, 1 for somewhat tired).
  • How you felt the entire week?: An overall assessment of the person’s mood for the full week, maybe graded on a scale (1 being extremely negative and 5 being very positive).
